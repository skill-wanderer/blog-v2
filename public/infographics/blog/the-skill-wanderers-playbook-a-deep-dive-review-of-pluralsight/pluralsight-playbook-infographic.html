8 Golden Rules for Maximizing Value

1

Wait for Major Sales

Black Friday deals can cut annual subscription by half. Less frequent than Udemy but worth the wait.

2

Start with IQ Test

Establish baseline and get personalized learning paths. Don't start randomly watching courses.

3

Live in the Labs

Premium subscription is wasted on videos alone. Real learning happens in hands-on environments.

4

Architectural Understanding

Use "Big Picture" courses for high-level concepts, not just code-along projects.

5

Track Learning Externally

Platform tracking is buggy post-merger. Use your own calendar to schedule and track progress.

6

Quick Tech Overviews

Use 1.5x speed for technologies similar to what you know. Rapid grasp of basics and differences.

7

Parallel Platform Learning

When concepts don't click, immediately switch to other platforms for different perspectives.

8

Work-Study Supplement

Watch relevant courses after hitting work roadblocks. Patch knowledge gaps discovered at work.
